aside elements

any aside element with a parent article or parent section will be floated right. it will have a width of 35% of its parent container. you can use asides however you want. place figures, images or text in them or whatever. just do not nest asides

text formatting

of course you style text. use the bring attention to tag or the strong tag to bring attention to or bold the text, respectively. additionally, consider using the i or the emphasize elements to emphasize text or to identify abornal text.

consider using the unarticulated annotation element, which squigly underlines words. use the inline quotation to insert brief quotes

use the small tags to create smaller text. use the mark element to highlight words for reference or notational purposes. press the space key to move down
